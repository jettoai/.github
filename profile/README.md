## [Tally](https://github.com/jettoai/tally) · AI subscription dashboard, open source

Every AI subscription you own, at a glance, in your menu bar: multi-account Claude and Codex
quota windows side by side, a pooled fleet gauge with a pace forecast, and a launcher that
starts every session on the account whose quota goes furthest, then resumes the conversation
on the next account if you hit a cap. Native Swift, MIT.
